Park Saebyul - Sky High (Dopefeel Remix)

25 Jun 2017 on Music and Dopefeel

We are Dopefeel, Doyeon Kwak and Jongpil Lee (we made the team name ‘Dopefeel’ by combining Do from Doyeon and pil from Jongpil).
We are pleased to release Dopefeel’s first work. We remixed the song “Sky High” by Park Saebyul, a professional singer.
We’d appreciate it so much if you could enjoy and share it. (Saebyul, Doyeon and I are all at Graduate School of Culture Technology, KAIST)
